Why the InBody scan matters: A regular scale can't tell muscle from fat — it's entirely possible to gain muscle and lose fat at the same time and see the exact same number, which is exactly the kind of thing that makes people quit for the wrong reason. InBody uses a quick, painless, clinically-validated scan (about 60 seconds, nothing invasive) to show your actual muscle mass, fat mass, and water distribution — the real picture your coach uses to know if your program is working and adjust it before you plateau.
